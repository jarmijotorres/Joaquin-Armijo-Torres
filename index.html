About me

I'm a young astronomer, currently doing my PhD at the Institute of Computational Cosmology (ICC) and the Institute of Data Science (IDAS) at Durham University. I like science, music, video games and travelling in that order.

My interest is to understand the large scale structures of the Universe to study cosmology, alternatives models of gravity and galaxy evolution. Also part of the Centres of Doctoral Training (CDT) program, to give different perspectives on machine learning from the academia.
